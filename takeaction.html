Take Action

Learn how to help and where to look

What can you do?

You can contribute to the conservation of coastal ecosystems from your home by reducing plastic consumption, participating in beach cleanup activities, supporting environmental organizations, and promoting environmental education in your community.

Examples of some organizations with similar goals:

Scuba Dogs Society is an environmental organization that cultivates education in action to create a culture of harmony with nature. They clean the coasts, green areas, and bodies of water in Puerto Rico.

Sociedad Ambiente Marino (SAM) is a scientific and community-based, non-profit environmental organization that aims to foster the conservation and restoration of marine resources. SAM fulfills its mission through a combination of efforts and services, including scientific research, coral reef restoration, marine mitigation, public education, outreach, and advocacy.

AmandOceano promotes actions to preserve Puerto Rico's marine ecosystems through education and activities for a healthy ocean for the marine life and the communities that depend on it.
